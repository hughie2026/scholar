01 / About

Biography

Hughie Tao is an Associate Professor at the Macao University of Tourism and a Fellow of the Royal Society for Public Health (FRSPH). His work lies at the intersection of lifestyle medicine, data science, travel medicine, and public health systems. He is dedicated to developing open research tools that lower barriers to rigorous, reproducible scholarship.

Beyond teaching, he maintains a growing collection of web-based research platforms that support data collection, analysis, and dissemination for fellow scholars.

Education

  • DrPH — Epidemiology and Biostatistics, Macau University of Science and Technology
  • PhD (Online) — Computer Science, University of Southern Mississippi
  • MPH — Macau University of Science and Technology

Research Interests

  • Lifestyle Medicine
  • Data Science & AI
  • Travel Medicine
  • Public Health Systems
02 / Research

Research Focus

Applied Data Analytics

Methodological work on bringing modern statistical and computational techniques to bear on questions in hospitality, tourism, and the social sciences.

Open Research Infrastructure

Building accessible web tools that handle the unglamorous parts of empirical research — sampling, instrumentation, processing — so investigators can focus on substance.

Pedagogy & Knowledge Transfer

Translating research methods into teaching materials and student-facing platforms that make quantitative literacy approachable.
